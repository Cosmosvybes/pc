# 30-Day Launch Plan: ParentingCertainty

**Goal:** Acquire 100 Active Users.
**Strategy:** "The Vulnerable Builder" (Value-First, Low-Promotion).

---

##  Week 1: The "Soft Launch" (Reddit)
*Focus: Getting your first 10 users and feedback.*

*   **Day 1 (Tuesday/Wednesday best):**
    *   **Action:** Post the "Vulnerable Parent" story (from `marketing_strategy.md`) to **r/Parenting**.
    *   **Rule:** Do NOT put the link in the main post body if the subreddit rules are strict. Put it in the *comments* or say "Link in bio/DM me".
    *   **Engagement:** Reply to EVERY comment within 1 hour. Verified builders get 10x trust.

*   **Day 2:**
    *   **Action:** Monitor specific keywords on Reddit using [f5bot.com](https://f5bot.com) (It's free).
    *   **Keywords (Copy Paste these):**
        *   **Direct Pain:** "yelled at my son", "yelled at my daughter", "lost my temper", "screaming at kids", "scared my child", "anger management parenting".
        *   **The Feeling:** "mom guilt yelling", "worst mom ever", "worst dad ever", "hate myself for yelling", "parenting burnout", "at my wits end".
        *   **The Trigger:** "overstimulated mom", "noise sensitivity", "toddler won't listen", "bedtime battle", "sensory overload parent".
        *   **Niche:** "ADHD rage", "autistic burnout parenting", "gentle parenting is hard".
    *   **Reply:** When someone posts about yelling, comment: *"I struggled with this too. I actually built a free tool to give me a script in that moment."* (Soft sell).

*   **Day 3:**
    *   **Action:** Post to **r/daddit**.
    *   **Angle:** "Fellow Dads, I built a tool to stop myself from yelling." (Dads love practical tools).

*   **Day 4-7:**
    *   **Action:** Fix critical bugs found by these first users.
    *   **Action:** DM every user who signed up (if you have emails via Lemon Squeezy) asking for 1 feedback item.

## Week 2: Content Repurposing (Short Video)
*Focus: Visualizing the "Regulation Station".*

*   **Day 8:**
    *   **Action:** Record a screen capture of the **"Coherent Breathing"** circle on your phone.
    *   **Post:** Post to **TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts**.
    *   **Caption:** "POV: You're about to lose it, but you press this button." (No talking needed, just the visual + Brown Noise audio).
    *   **Hashtags:** #gentleparenting #adhdparenting #overstimulation

*   **Day 10:**
    *   **Action:** Crosspost the video to **r/adhdwomen** or **r/ADHD** dealing with "Overstimulation". 
    *   **Title:** "I made a visual breathing tool for when I get overstimulated by my kids."

*   **Day 12:**
    *   **Action:** Create a "Crisis Script" carousel for Instagram/Pinterest.
    *   **Slide 1:** "Stop Yelling."
    *   **Slide 2:** "Say this instead:"
    *   **Slide 3:** *[Screenshot of your app's script]*
    *   **Slide 4:** "Link in bio to get the full script."
